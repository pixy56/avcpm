# Andrej Karpathy

## Role

AI researcher and engineer. Originated the **LLM Wiki** pattern in April 2026 via a viral GitHub Gist, proposing a new approach to LLM-powered knowledge management as an alternative to traditional RAG.

## Key Contribution

The LLM Wiki pattern: instead of retrieving raw document chunks on every query, an LLM incrementally compiles sources into a persistent, interlinked markdown wiki with `[[wikilinks]]`.
